Once you have installed and configured Ansible, you will want to
move on to doing basic administrative tasks on the BIG-IP.

The following recipes target a common subset of work that people
typically undertake when configuring BIG-IP devices. Among the
various recipes included in this chapter are means to create pools
and virtual servers, provision modules, and manage users and partitions.

We will also see recipes for modules that are used in nearly every playbook.
